A timer based on the Pomodoro Technique


The Pomodoro Technique


a method that can "help anyone to focus."


  1. Choose a task to be accomplished.
  2. Set the time on appropriate field for Work and Break (25min work and 5 min break are the defaults).
  3. Work on the task until work session is over.
  4. Take a short break (Rest timer will notify when it's over).
  5. Every 4 Pomodoros take a longer break (30 min).
